INTRODUCTION
.      This application in chambers is scheduled before Your Lordship on July 26, 2023
at 2:00 p.m., on a motion for summary judgment on the evidence dismissing the
proceeding pursuant to the Limitation of Actions Act.' Micaela Sheppard and I
represent the respondents, Arichat Metal Fabrication Ltd. (“Arichat Metal”) and
John Boudreau, the moving parties of the motion (together, “AMF Respondents”).
The applicants are represented by Michelle Kelly and Lisa Delaney. The co-
respondent, Gregory Boucher, and the third party, 1766134 Nova Scotia Limited
(“176 NSL”), are represented by Adam Harris.
.      The AMF Respondents had filed a motion for summary judgment on the evidence
on April 1, 2021 (amended April 8, 2021; further amended May 24, 2023). In
support, the AMF Respondents filed a brief on April 8, 2021 and the applicants
filed a brief on May 20, 2021. Since that time, however, the parties have dealt with
a motion for production and discovery, which resulted in the production of crucial
new evidence to the AMF Respondents which we now rely on in support of the
present motion.
